Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 April 2026

1. Introduction

Quantex Scanner ("we", "us", "our") operates the website app.cibronix.com and the Telegram bot @QuantexScannerBot. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

By using Quantex Scanner, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you sign up for a free trial or paid subscription, we may collect the following information:

Personal Information: Your name, phone number, email address, and Telegram username as provided during signup via our Telegram bot or payment gateway.

Payment Information: Transaction ID, payment amount, and subscription plan. We do not store your credit card numbers, UPI PINs, or bank account details — all payments are processed securely by our third-party payment gateway (Instamojo).

Usage Data: Telegram bot interaction data such as commands sent (/trial, /status) and your Telegram user ID for group management purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Service Delivery: To manage your subscription, add you to the Telegram subscriber group and WhatsApp community, and deliver daily pre-market reports.

Communication: To send subscription expiry reminders, renewal alerts, and important service updates via Telegram.

Subscription Management: To track active subscriptions, process renewals, and manage group access (adding/removing members based on subscription status).

We do not use your information for advertising, marketing to third parties, or any purpose unrelated to delivering the Quantex Scanner service.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your subscription data is stored securely in our private GitHub repository with restricted access. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Payment processing is handled entirely by Instamojo, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. We never have access to your card details, UPI PIN, or banking credentials.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is only shared with:

Payment Gateway (Instamojo): To process your subscription payments securely.

Telegram: Your Telegram username and user ID are used via the Telegram Bot API to manage group membership and send notifications.

We will never share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any unrelated third parties.

6. Data Retention

We retain your subscription data for as long as your account is active, plus 90 days after expiry for resubscription convenience. After 90 days of inactivity, your personal data may be permanently deleted from our systems.

Payment records are retained for accounting and tax compliance purposes as required by Indian law.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you by messaging our Telegram bot with /status.

Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate personal information.

Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data by contacting us via Telegram. We will remove your data within 7 working days.

Opt-out: You may cancel your subscription at any time. Upon expiry, you will be automatically removed from all groups.

8. Cookies & Tracking

Our website is a static page hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not use cookies, analytics trackers, or any third-party tracking scripts. We do not collect browsing data or IP addresses.
